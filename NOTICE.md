# NOTICE — Takedown Process

TAI KIT is an open 3D model collection. While every item is a generic
utilitarian kitchen object and names are re-generated to contain no brand,
SKU, or source identifier, we recognize that edge cases can still arise.

## If you believe a model infringes on rights you hold

Please open a GitHub issue with the following:

- **Item ID** (e.g., `tk_0042`)
- **Nature of concern** (e.g., trademark visible in baked-in texture,
  distinctive industrial design attributable to a specific designer,
  personal likeness, etc.)
- **Your relationship** to the rights claimed (rights holder,
  authorized representative, legal counsel, etc.)

## What we will do

- Acknowledge the issue within **7 days**
- Review the specific item in good faith
- Remove, re-texture, or otherwise address the item if the concern is valid
- Publish a short note in `CHANGELOG.md` recording the outcome

We err on the side of removal when the concern is reasonable, even when
not strictly legally required. This release is a small-scale test release
— being responsive matters more than being maximally defensive.

## What we cannot do

- Respond to concerns that do not identify a specific `tk_XXXX` ID
- Enter into direct licensing negotiations (items are MIT-licensed as-is;
  we have no commercial interest in any specific item)
